Frequently Asked Questions

Hydro jetting is a plumbing method that uses high-pressure water to clear out blockages and buildup in pipes.

A specialized nozzle attached to a high-pressure hose releases a powerful stream of water into the pipes, effectively breaking up debris and flushing out blockages.

Hydro jetting is safe for most types of pipes, including PVC, copper, and steel. However, it may not be suitable for older or fragile pipes.

Some benefits of hydro jetting include effectively removing stubborn blockages, preventing future clogs, and improving overall plumbing system performance.

The frequency of hydro jetting depends on various factors such as the age of the plumbing system, the presence of recurring clogs, and the type of debris typically found in the pipes. It is recommended to consult a professional plumber for personalized advice.
