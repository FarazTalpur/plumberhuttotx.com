What is Hydro Jetting?

Hydro jetting is a state-of-the-art cleaning process that uses high-pressure water jets to clear out blockages and debris from plumbing pipes. Unlike traditional snaking methods that merely punch a hole through the clog, hydro jetting completely eliminates the blockage, scouring the interior of pipes to prevent future complications. This method is not only more effective but also environmentally friendly, as it dispenses with the need for toxic chemical drain cleaners.

Key Benefits of Hydro Jetting

  • Comprehensive Cleaning : Hydro jetting cleans the entire pipe wall, leaving no debris or residue behind. This thorough cleaning approach minimizes the likelihood of recurring blockages.
  • Eco-Friendly Process : Utilizing only high-pressure water, hydro jetting avoids harmful chemicals, making it safe for the environment and your plumbing system.
  • Preventive Maintenance : Regular hydro jetting can serve as a preventive measure, maintaining optimal flow in your plumbing system and reducing the need for emergency interventions.

Is hydro jetting safe for all types of pipes?

Yes, hydro jetting is safe for most types of plumbing systems. Our team assesses each situation to determine the proper pressure settings, ensuring the safety and integrity of your pipes.

How often should hydro jetting be performed?

The frequency of hydro jetting depends on the usage and age of the plumbing system. For optimal maintenance, it is recommended annually for residential homes and biannually for commercial establishments.

Can hydro jetting remove tree roots?

Yes, hydro jetting is effective in removing tree roots invading your sewer lines. However, to prevent regrowth, the source of the roots will need addressing afterward.
