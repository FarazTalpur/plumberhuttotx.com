Why Kitchen Drains Get Clogged

Understanding the causes of kitchen drain clogs can help in preventing future incidents. Here are the most common culprits behind pesky blockages:

  • Grease and Fats : When washed down a drain, these substances can solidify, resulting in stubborn clogs.
  • Food Scraps : Foods that expand with water, such as rice and pasta, can block drains when improperly disposed.
  • Soap Scum : Over time, soap can combine with minerals in the water, forming a hard residue that blocks pipes.
  • Foreign Objects : Accidentally dropped utensils or small kitchen items can obstruct the flow of water.

Emergency Tips Before Help Arrives

While waiting for the plumber to arrive, there are a few DIY tactics that homeowners in Hutto can employ to temporarily manage a clogged drain:

  • Boiling Water : Pour a pot of boiling water into the sink to dissolve mild grease or soap clogs.
  • Baking Soda & Vinegar Mix : Pour a cup of baking soda followed by a cup of vinegar. Cover the drain and let it sit for 15 minutes before flushing with hot water.
  • Plunger Use : A plunger can provide enough force to dislodge smaller blockages.

These methods are temporary measures and should not replace professional evaluations and interventions, especially during emergencies.
