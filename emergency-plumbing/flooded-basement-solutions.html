Frequently Asked Questions

A flooded basement can be caused by heavy rainfall, melting snow, clogged gutters, or a burst pipe.

You can prevent basement flooding by installing a sump pump, grading the land away from the foundation, and ensuring proper gutter maintenance.

If your basement floods, you should first prioritize safety, then try to identify the source of the flooding and contact a professional for assistance.

Depending on the extent of the damage, you may be able to clean up a flooded basement yourself. However, it is recommended to hire professionals for thorough restoration.

The cost of fixing a flooded basement can vary greatly depending on the extent of the damage and the necessary repairs. It is best to get a professional assessment for an accurate cost estimate.
