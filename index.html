Maintenance Tips to Prevent Plumbing Emergencies

While some emergencies are unavoidable, regular maintenance can help prevent many common plumbing issues. Here are some tips to keep your plumbing system in top shape:

Regular Inspections

Schedule periodic inspections to identify and fix minor issues before they become major problems. Our team can conduct thorough assessments to ensure your plumbing systemâ€™s integrity.

Keep Drains Clear

Avoid pouring grease or oil down the drain as they can solidify and cause blockages. Use strainers to trap food particles and hair.

Test Water Pressure

Excessive water pressure can strain pipes and cause leaks. Use a pressure gauge to test your homeâ€™s water pressure and keep it within the recommended range.

Flush Water Heater Annually

Sediment buildup can hamper your heater's efficiency. Flushing it annually can help maintain its functionality and extend its life.

Familiarize yourself with your home's water shut-off valves to minimize damage in case of a leak.
