Tips to Prevent Plumbing Emergencies

While weâ€™re here to help when emergencies occur, prevention is always the best approach. Here are some tips to help you avoid common plumbing issues:

  • Regular Maintenance: Schedule regular inspections and maintenance for your plumbing system to catch potential issues before they become emergencies.

  • Avoid Chemical Drain Cleaners: These can damage your pipes in the long term. Use natural alternatives like baking soda and vinegar or call a professional for stubborn clogs.

  • Watch What You Flush: Avoid flushing anything other than toilet paper to prevent blockages.

  • Proper Insulation: Insulate water pipes during winter to prevent them from freezing and bursting.

  • Monitor Water Pressure: High water pressure can lead to pipe damage; install a pressure regulator to maintain safe levels.
