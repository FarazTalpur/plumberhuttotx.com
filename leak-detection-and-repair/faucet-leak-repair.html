Common Causes of Faucet Leaks

  • Worn-out washers : These rubberized components degrade over time, leading to leaks.
  • Corroded valve seats : Corrosion from sediment can create leaks in the faucet's base.
  • Damaged O-rings : O-rings, crucial for cartridge-style faucets, often wear out and require replacement.

Benefits of Timely Repairs

  1. Cost Efficiency : A leaky faucet can waste gallons of water, increasing utility bills. Prompt repairs are a cost-effective solution.
  2. Prevention of Structural Damage : Persistent leaks can lead to mold growth and water damage in cabinetry or on floors.
  3. Enhanced Property Value : Well-maintained plumbing systems contribute positively to a home's market value.
