Understanding Tap Issues

Common Tap Problems

  • Leaking Taps: A common problem that can lead to higher water bills and water wastage.
  • Low Water Pressure: Often caused by internal build-up or issues in the water supply line.
  • Noisy Taps: May indicate air in the pipes or worn-out components.
  • Stiff or Jammed Taps: Usually due to mineral build-up or aging mechanisms.
  • Dripping Faucets: Can waste gallons of water annually if ignored.
  • Temperature Regulation Problems: Often caused by failed mixers or algorithms in electronic taps.

Causes of Tap Malfunctions

  • Wear and Tear: Daily use leads to gradual wear of parts.
  • Mineral Build-up: Hard water leads to accumulation affecting functionality.
  • Faulty Installation: Poor initial setup can lead to ongoing issues.
  • Corrosion: Older taps may suffer from corrosion causing leaks or blockage.
