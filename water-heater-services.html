Types of Water Heaters We Service

Tank Water Heaters

Traditional tank water heaters are a common choice for many homes. We offer expert repair and maintenance services to ensure these units operate at peak efficiency.

  • Leak Repair : Promptly address any leaks to prevent water damage and maintain the heater's efficiency.
  • Sediment Flushing : Regular maintenance to clear sediment buildup prolongs the lifespan and boosts energy efficiency.

Tankless Water Heaters

Tankless or on-demand water heaters are known for their energy efficiency. Our team is adept at troubleshooting and resolving issues specific to these systems.

  • Flow Rate Adjustment : Ensure your system is calibrated for optimal performance and energy savings.
  • Descaling : Prevent mineral buildup, which can reduce efficiency and lifespan, with routine descaling services.

Hybrid Water Heaters

Hybrid water heaters combine traditional tank and heat pump technology, providing energy-efficient heating. We offer specialized services for these advanced systems.

  • Component Testing : Regular checks to ensure all components are functioning correctly for continued efficiency.
  • System Calibration : Fine-tuning settings to optimize energy savings without sacrificing performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common signs include inconsistent water temperature, low hot water pressure, unusual noises from the heater, leaks around the unit, and cloudy or rusty hot water.

It's generally recommended to have your water heater professionally inspected and serviced once a year to ensure optimal performance and longevity.

Most traditional tank water heaters last about 10-15 years, while tankless models can last 20 years or more with proper maintenance.

While some minor issues like thermostat adjustments can be managed by homeowners, it is recommended to hire a professional for complex repairs or gas-related issues to ensure safety and proper handling.

A tank water heater stores and heats a large reservoir of water, while a tankless water heater heats water on demand, offering energy savings and an unlimited supply of hot water.
